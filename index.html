Steve Jobs

24/02/1955 - 5/10/2011

Steven Paul Jobs was an American business magnate, industrial designer, investor, and media proprietor. He was the chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), and co-founder of Apple Inc.

  • Birth

    24th Feb 1955

    Steven Paul was born in San Francisco.

  • Start of Apple Inc.

    1st Apr 1976

    Apple Computer Inc. is incorporated by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ron Wayne.

  • "Hello, I am Macintosh"

    24th Jan 1984

    Macintosh is launched in great fanfare at Apple's annual shareholder meeting..

  • Resignation

    17 Sep 1985

    Steve Jobs resigns from Apple and starts NeXT with five other refugees from Apple.

  • Rebuilding Apple

    Jul 1997

    Steve Jobs is named interim CEO in his place and installs his NeXT executive team at the top of Apple.

  • Say hello to iMac

    6 May 1998

    Steve Jobs introduces Apple's revolutionary iMac at the Flint Center auditorium in Cupertino, 14 years after he had introduced the Macintosh at that same place.

  • Steve Jobs becomes Apple CEO

    5 Jan 2000

    Steve Jobs drops the 'interim' in his title and officially becomes Apple's CEO. He also demoes Mac OS X's revolutionary Aqua interface to a bewildered audience.

  • A Thousand Songs. In Your Pocket

    23 Oct 2001

    Steve Jobs unveils iPod at a small media event on the company's campus.

  • The iPod Revolution

    6 Jan 2004

    Steve unveils the iPod mini and the iLife suite at Macworld. The iPod mini will soon become the world's best-selling MP3 player and truly establish Apple as a consumer electronics powerhouse.

  • Welcome to iPhone

    9 Jan 2007

    Steve Jobs introduces iPhone and its revolutionary touch-screen interface. He also introduces Apple TV and announces the company's name change from Apple Computer Inc. to Apple Inc. to better reflect its new nature.

  • The App Revolution

    6 Mar 2008

    Apple announces it will open the iPhone platform to outside developers with the App Store.

  • "iPad Introduction"

    27 Jan 2010

    After months of wild rumors, Steve Jobs unveils iPad, 'the biggest thing Apple's ever done'. The tablet runs the same operating system as iPhone.

  • The Last Keynote

    6 Jun 2011

    A frail Steve Jobs unveils Apple's cloud offering, iCloud, a centerpiece in Apple's product offering for decades to come..

  • Final Resignation

    24 Aug 2011

    Steve Jobs resigns as CEO of Apple.

  • Death

    5 Oct 2011

    Steve Jobs dies at home, surrounded by his family.
